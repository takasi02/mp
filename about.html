JUTE PRODUCTS

Jute craft is derived from the fibre of a reed-like plant. Jute is used chiefly to make cloth for wrapping bales of raw cotton, and to make sacks and coarse cloth. The fibers are also woven into curtains, chair coverings, carpets, area rugs. The fibers are used alone or blended with other types of fiber to make ropes.Jute is widely used because of its low cost
